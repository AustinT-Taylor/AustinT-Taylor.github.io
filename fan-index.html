Facts about David Neubauer

Facts

  • Member of the LSU squad for three years after winning a spot on the team in 2000 … Gave the Tigers depth on the offensive line, while also serving as a vital member of the LSU scout team.
  • Two-year letterman in football … Made All-District and Academic All-State his senior season … Helped lead his team to consecutive District Championships … Was coached in high school by Rusty Price.
  • Played Offensive guard at LSU.
