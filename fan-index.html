Facts about the Las Vegas Golden Knights

Facts

  • The team was established on June 22, 2016.
  • The Golden Knights play their home games at T-Mobile Arena in Paradise, Nevada, which has a capacity of 17,500.
  • The Golden Knights won their first Stanley Cup in 2023, defeating the Florida Panthers.
